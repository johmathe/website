Johan Mathe

I am a renaissance engineer, mad scientist and entrepreneur interested in understanding the impact of climate change and weather on a plethora of metrics and systems. My background is applied mathematics and distributed systems. So far I have applied these skills to Flying Robots, Healthcare, Plasma Physics and Energy processing.

I co-founded Froglabs, where we are building algorithms and infrastructure to understand the impact of weather 1000x faster than the current state of the art.

Cultivating playfulness is in my opinion critical. With a dedicated crew of tinkerers, we enjoy building useless things. One of our most recent completed projects was a remote-controlled flame-throwing inflatable kayak. We also tried to reproduce zero and negative gravity conditions in a tiny aerobatics airplane.
