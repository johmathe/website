The Albatros L-39 is a versatile trainer, similar to the Alpha-Jet in terms of performance.

Specs (L-39C)

Data from Jane's All The World's Aircraft 1988–89

General characteristics

  • Crew: 2

  • Length: 12.13 m (39 ft 9½ in)

  • Wingspan: 9.46 m (31 ft 0½ in)

  • Height: 4.77 m (15 ft 7¾ in)

  • Wing area: 18.8 m² (202 ft²)

  • Airfoil: NACA 64A012 mod

  • Empty weight: 3,455 kg (7,617 lb)

Performance

  • Service ceiling: 11,000 m (36,100 ft)

  • Thrust/weight: 0.37
